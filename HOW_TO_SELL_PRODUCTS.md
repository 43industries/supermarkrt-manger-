## ğŸ¯ **Quick Start: How to Sell**

### **Step 1: Go to POS (Point of Sale)**
1. **Login** to the system
   - Username: `admin` or `cashier`
   - Password: `admin123` or `cashier123`

2. **Click on "POS" tab** (usually at the top menu)
   - Look for tabs/buttons: **Dashboard**, **POS**, **Products**, **Customers**, **Reports**
   - Click **"POS"** or **"Point of Sale"**

---

### **Step 2: Add Products to Cart**

You have **3 ways** to add products:

#### **Method 1: Search by Name** (Best for products WITHOUT barcodes)
1. Find the **search box** (usually at the top)
2. **Type the product name** (e.g., "Rice", "Milk", "Tomatoes")
3. Products matching your search will appear
4. **Click on the product** to add it to cart
5. Or press **Enter** after typing the name

#### **Method 2: Click Product Cards**
1. Products are displayed as **cards** or in a **list**
2. **Click on any product card**
3. It will be added to your cart automatically

#### **Method 3: Scan Barcode** (For products WITH barcodes)
1. Find the **barcode scanner field** (usually highlighted in blue)
2. **Scan the barcode** with your scanner
3. Product is automatically added to cart
4. Or **type the barcode** if no scanner

---

### **Step 3: Review Your Cart**

After adding products, you'll see:
- **Cart/Shopping Cart** section (usually on the right side)
- List of items you're selling
- **Quantity** for each item (you can increase/decrease)
- **Total price** at the bottom

**To change quantity:**
- Click **+** or **-** buttons
- Or type the quantity directly

**To remove an item:**
- Click **Remove** or **X** button next to the item

---

### **Step 4: Select Payment Method**

Choose how the customer is paying:

1. **Cash**
   - Click **"Cash"** button
   - Enter the amount received from customer
   - System calculates change automatically

2. **Card**
   - Click **"Card"** button
   - Process card payment on your terminal
   - Click **"Complete Sale"**

3. **M-Pesa** (if available)
   - Click **"M-Pesa"** button
   - Enter customer phone number
   - Click **"Complete Sale"**

---

### **Step 5: Complete the Sale**

1. **Review the total** in the cart
2. **Click "Complete Sale"** or **"Checkout"** button (usually big green button)
3. **Receipt will be generated** automatically
4. **Stock is automatically updated**
5. Cart is cleared, ready for next sale
