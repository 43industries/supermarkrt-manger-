## üéØ Where to Add Products

### **Step 1: Go to Products Management**

1. **Login** to the system
   - Admin: `admin` / `admin123`
   - Cashier: `cashier` / `cashier123`

2. **Click on "Products"** in the navigation menu (top menu bar)

3. **Click the "Add Product" button** (usually a green or blue button at the top of the products list)

---

### **Step 2: Fill in the Product Form**

When the product form appears, you'll see these fields:

#### **Required Fields** (Must fill):
- ‚úÖ **Product Name** - e.g., "Rice 5kg", "Fresh Tomatoes"
- ‚úÖ **Cost Price** - How much you bought it for
- ‚úÖ **Selling Price** - How much you sell it for

#### **Optional Fields** (Can leave empty):
- ‚ö†Ô∏è **Barcode** - **You can leave this EMPTY!**
- Category - e.g., "Grains", "Vegetables", "Dairy"
- Stock - Current quantity in stock
- Reorder Level - When to order more (default: 10)
- Supplier - Supplier name

---

### **Step 3: Save the Product**

1. **Fill in the required fields** (Name, Cost Price, Selling Price)
2. **Leave Barcode field EMPTY** (or just skip it)
3. **Click "Save" or "Add Product"** button

**That's it!** Your product will be saved without a barcode.

---

## üîç How to Use Products Without Barcodes

### **Option 1: Search by Name**
- Type the product name in the search box
- The system will find products by name (not just barcode)
- Click on the product to add it to cart

### **Option 2: Direct Selection**
- Browse the products list
- Click on any product to add it to cart
- Barcode is not needed!
